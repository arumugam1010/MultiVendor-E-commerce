COMMON QUESTIONS

Merchant & Logistics FAQ

Answers to common questions about payouts, escrow holding times, and platform parameters.

How long are transaction funds held in SecureEscrow Pay?

+

Payments processed through our escrow module are held securely in localized node pools. Funds are released instantly to your seller balance as soon as the logistics carrier tracking confirms product delivery or when the customer registers positive execution signals.

Are there any limits on developer API integrations?

+

Basic Merchant tier has access to basic checkout redirects. The Enterprise Suite tier enjoys unlimited, rate-free API tokens to synchronize order, inventory, and analytics tables directly with high-performance ERP systems (such as SAP or custom nodes).

What coverage models does Stackly Express operate under?

+

We currently partner with regional hyper-local distribution channels and courier services to support express same-day packaging and drop-off. Warehouse allocations are managed via high-density fulfillment nodes situated in prime commercial areas.
